Drone with self-made flight controller

This project aims to develop an affordable and scalable drone system for large-scale surveillance in ports and airports, addressing the security needs of the Indian government. By utilizing cost-effective hardware and software, such as coreless motors and economical Electronic Speed Controllers (ESCs), the project ensures low-cost production without sacrificing performance. NRF modules are employed to replace traditional radio control systems, enabling simpler, cheaper remote control and field updates. The focus is on creating low-cost, long-range drones with enhanced stability and extended flight times, making them ideal for surveillance missions. This initiative has the potential to revolutionize surveillance capabilities, significantly enhancing security and safety in critical areas. By making advanced drone technology viable and accessible, the project promises to provide an effective solution for the Indian government’s security measures, ensuring efficient monitoring and protection of vital infrastructures.
